Going Through...

Originally born and raised in Beijing, China, Jamie moved to Chicago, US for her college education in 2021. This site collects her photography works from 2018-2022. All the pictures here exemplify her lifestyles and moods in these two different cities. Feel free to explore around by clicking on the eyes!

*This site is under continuous construction, as Jamie continues her journey in the world.
