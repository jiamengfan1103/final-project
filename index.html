Going Through...

Originally born and raised in Beijing, China, Jamie moved to Chicago, US for her college education in 2021. This site collects her photography works from 2019 to 2022. All the pictures here exemplify her lifestyles and moods in these two different cities. Feel free to explore around by clicking on the eyes!

*This site is under continuous construction, as Jamie continues her journey in the world.

*Special thanks to Professor Briz, who has taught Jamie how to write code in HTML and CSS, and also to WDQ, who has always inspired Jamie.
